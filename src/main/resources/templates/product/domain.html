.com

The .com TLD is the most widely used and recognized TLD in the world. It is often used for commercial websites and is considered the most professional choice for businesses.

.org

The .org TLD is typically used by non-profit organizations and educational institutions. It is often considered a more trustworthy and credible choice for these types of websites.

.info

The .info TLD is a generic TLD that can be used for any type of website. It is often used for informational websites, such as blogs and news sites.
